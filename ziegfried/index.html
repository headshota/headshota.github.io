What We Do

Planning and Strategy:
Creating a comprehensive business plan for your entry or expansion in Eastern Europe.

Legal and Regulatory Issues:
Determining export/import licensing needs, understanding and complying with product standards, certification requirements for each country.

Documentation and Product Requirements:
Learning about import/export documents, invoices, packing lists, and certificates of origin; verifying the tariff rates, as well as any import fees for a particular market.

Country and Industry Reports:
Getting accurate and detailed information - from country and industry market reports.

Customized Market Research:
Individualized reports on global markets - discovering which factors influence prospective customers, identifying your competitors, and obtaining pricing for comparable products.

Contact Lists:
Finding pre-qualified buyers, distributors, licensees, and other business partners with the assistance of our representatives.

Partner Search:
Identify potential partners and get detailed company reports.

Background Reports:
Learning about potential partners from our representatives working locally.

In-Country Promotions and Trade Shows:
Promoting your products and distributing your marketing literature at local trade shows.

Our Mission

At Zigfried we strive to give opportunities to companies to expand their clientele and to spread their business to new territories.
